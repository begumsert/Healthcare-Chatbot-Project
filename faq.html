Health Chatbot 101: Everything You Need to Know


Q1: How do I start using the healthcare chatbot?

A1: Simply click on the 'Chatbot' at the top-right corner of your screen and type your question. Our chatbot is ready to assist you with your healthcare queries.

Q2: Is the healthcare chatbot free to use?

A2: Yes, our chatbot service is completely free and designed to provide you with quick, reliable health information anytime.

Q3: Can the healthcare chatbot diagnose my health conditions?

A3: Our chatbot provides information based on the symptoms you describe, but it's not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis. Please consult a healthcare provider for an official diagnosis.

Q4: What kind of questions can I ask the healthcare chatbot?

A4: You can ask about common health concerns, symptoms you’re experiencing, general advice on health conditions, and guidance on when to seek medical care.

Q5: What should I do if the chatbot doesn’t understand my question?

A5: If the chatbot is unable to understand your query, try rephrasing your question or use simpler terms. If you still need assistance, our customer support team is here to help.

Q6: Is the information provided by the chatbot accurate and up-to-date?

A6: Our chatbot is programmed with information that's regularly reviewed and updated by healthcare professionals to ensure accuracy and timeliness
